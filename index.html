BIO

Edens


Born in Gonaives, Haiti, Edens Saint Fleur is a composed and creative designer and marketer. However, above all else Edens is a CURIOUS person, as interested in the process behind something as the finished product. Now in his third year at The University of Connecticut as a member of the Digital Media and Design program Edens has worked as an assistant project manager in the Beachball agency and as a social media manager for a vehicle wrapping franchise in the UK. Edens plans to continue to develop and cultivate a varied skill set with the aim of producing the best work for his clients. Edens also loves Netflix.
